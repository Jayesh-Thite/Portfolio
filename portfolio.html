About me

I design with purpose, not just pixels.

I'm a UX Designer who believes great design starts with understanding people — their goals, frustrations, and the moments where a product either earns trust or loses it.

I work across the full design process: from discovery and research, through wireframes and prototyping, to high-fidelity UI and developer handoff. I've shipped 0-to-1 products, redesigned complex flows, and built design systems from scratch.
